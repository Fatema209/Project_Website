Located in Fujairah, 1.7 km from Umbrella Beach, Al Bahar Hotel & Resort provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre. Among the various facilities of this property are a shared lounge, a garden and a private beach area. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.

Al Bahar Hotel & Resort offers 5-star accommodation with a hot tub and children's playground. You can play table tennis at the accommodation, and car hire is available.

The property has an on-site sauna, hairdresser's and business centre.

Fujairah Mall is 5 km from Al Bahar Hotel & Resort. The nearest airport is Fujairah International Airport, 4 km from the resort.
