Set on a private beach, Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel is a heritage retreat that offers views of the Arabian Gulf and the city. It features luxurious accommodations with free WiFi, an outdoor pool and a gym.

Featuring a French balcony or terrace, all rooms and residences at Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel and Resort offer a sea or city view equipped with a flat-screen TV, a minibar and an electric kettle. The apartment includes a spacious living room.

With an array of restaurants and lounge options, as well as attentive 24-hour room service, the hotel serves a myriad of cuisines from around the world and offers a breakfast buffet every morning.

The hotel is a picturesque sanctuary conveniently located 22 km from Dubai International Airport and 11 km from Sharjah International Airport. An airport transfer service is available at extra cost upon request.
