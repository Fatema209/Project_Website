Details:

Located in Manama’s Diplomatic District, The Domain Bahrain offers modern accommodations. It features free Wi-Fi in all areas and meeting facilities are available upon request.

All air-conditioned suites are serviced by floor butlers. Each one offers a private entrance, a living room with a flat-screen TV and a minibar. The bathroom is fitted with a shower or bath and complimentary toiletries.

Guests can enjoy a daily breakfast at the hotel’s restaurant. Room service is available 24 hours upon request.

The Marina is 5 km away and Bahrain National Museum is 10 minutes away by car. Bahrain International Airport is a 15-minute drive from The Domain Bahrain.
