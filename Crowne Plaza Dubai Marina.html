Crowne Plaza Dubai Marina has 5 dining outlets, outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and bar in Dubai. Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a sun terrace. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.
