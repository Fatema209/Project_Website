The 5-star Venue Jeddah Corniche hotel is placed in the heart of Jeddah overlooking the beautiful Red Sea Riviera waterfront. Located only at a 15-minute away from the world’s tallest fountain, King Fahd’s fountain. A shopping adventure lies within 5-minute away at the Red-Sea mall, 20 minutes away from UNESCO World Heritage site of Al-Balad on the historical downtown. Located within walking distance from a playful water park, The-Beach.

The Venue offers all day business center, 3 dining outlets, 14 state-of-the-art meeting rooms, Jeddah’s only pillar-less wedding Hall Duniaty , kids club, beauty salon and a fitness club with 2 swimming pools.

At the Venue’s Le Voyageur, the main restaurant, an international cuisine is served as Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner buffets. Other dining options available at the Lobby, serving quick bites; and cocktails and casual food at Astro Lounge café, set at a magnificent view of the Red-Sea.

Guests can treat themselves at the spa and massage facilities with a pampering menu to relax and revitalize. It includes saunas, steam rooms and a Jacuzzi. There is also an outdoor swimming pool for men and an indoor pool for women.
