This 5-star hotel is directly attached to City Centre, the largest mall in Bahrain. A host of complimentary facilities such as swimming pool, infinity pool, fitness and spa facilities are available free of charge. Spa treatments can be booked at an additional charge.

Bahrain International Airport is a 15-minute drive away and Bahrain International Exhibition Centre 1.1 km. Local attractions include Al Fateh Mosque, Manama Souk and Bahrain National Museum. Wahooo! Indoor waterpark and a 20-cinema complex can be found in the attached mall.
