This 5-star deluxe hotel in the heart of Dammam’s business district boasts a luxurious wellness centre and several pools. 3 restaurants and a lounge bar offer varied dining opportunities.

About one km from the Sheraton Dammam Hotel & Convention Centre, Dammam Corniche features open-air art exhibitions. Al Shatea Shopping Mall is just steps from the hotel.

The rooms of the Sheraton Dammam Hotel & Convention Centre offer panoramic views of the Arabian Gulf. They are decorated in cream shades, with rich fabrics and luxurious furniture.

Sheraton Dammam’s wide dining offer includes international themed buffets, as well as Italian and Chino-Japanese specialities. The casual Vienna Café provides a variety of snacks, aromatic coffees and pastries.
